# DSA-Tracker-Striver

A beautifully designed, local-first progress tracker for the **Striver A2Z DSA Sheet**.

This interactive tracker helps you stay on top of your Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA) preparation by organizing problems into structured phases (Arrays, Binary Search, Trees, Graphs, DP, etc.).

## Features

- **Comprehensive Tracking:** Tracks hundreds of DSA problems logically grouped by topics and days.
- **Direct Links:** One-click access to problem statements on **LeetCode (LC)** and **GeeksforGeeks (GFG)**.
- **Local Storage:** Your progress is saved automatically in your browser's local storage—no login or backend required.
- **Progress Visualizations:** Global and phase-specific progress bars give you a clear view of your completion percentage.
- **Task Filtering:** Easily filter tasks by "All", "Pending", or "Done" to focus on your immediate goals.
- **Sleek UI:** Modern, responsive dark-mode interface built with HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript.

## How to use
Simply open the `dsa_tracker-2.html` file in your browser to start tracking your DSA journey!
